FLL RePlay 2020-2021 Season

How can we motivate people to exercise while being as modern as possible?

We were split on many different ideas for this season. But one thing was clear. Our project had to be technology-related.

Background Information

Some initial ideas that came to mind were making exercise available to all, and making exercise available to the disabled.

As we were researching, we observed many people were spending increased time indoors. After further research, we were able to determine that a majority of the time indoors was spent playing video games.

We immediately knew this was a brilliant route to go through for our project because it was a widespread problem during these times, and it has an overall bigger impact on the majority of the population.

About ReLive

ReLive offers a never-before-seen technology that can modify backgrounds and themes in your favorite video games! By making regular video games more relatable to you on a personal level.

We have talked to multiple fitness trainers and neurologists, and we have learned that exercising while looking at places associated with happy memories increases seratonin and dopamine levels in your brain by over 200x! In effect, this makes you happier.

Project

For the RePlay season, the Lightning Bots identified a method that is simple and scientifically proven to motivate people to exercise. Click here to try our project!

By utilizing positive and health-beneficial chemical reactions in the brain, the Lightning Bots motivate people to exercise while watching happy memories.

Meet Wallace.
Our robot, Wallace, has survived nearly three full years of hard usage. On one event, a team member dropped the robot from a height of 12 or so inches, and there was not a single sign of destruction.

Wallace has 4 motors, two of which are large motors and the other two are medium motors. It also has 3 sensors, two of which are color sensors and the last one is a gyro.
