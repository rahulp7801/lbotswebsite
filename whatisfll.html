First Lego League (FLL) is an international robotics program built on the foundations of STEM. The FLL competition has three main parts. Robot Game, Innovation Project, and Core Values. Each year, teams program an autonomous robot and identify solutions to real-world problems!

Teams are also assigned a different overarching topic to design and think for. For the 2020-2021 season, the topic in question was "RePlay". How can we help people become more motivated to exercise?

And most importantly, have fun!!!
Robot Game
Teams program a fully autonomous robot that will accomplish missions on a game mat, which is updated every year.
Innovation Project
We wanted our project to be very tech-savvy, affordable for the average person, and improve overall health while encouraging people to exercise.
Core Values
As we are an experienced team, we offer our knowledge to young rookie teams through mentoring. We are also volunteering to teach underpriveleged children in our community about STEM.
Lightning Bots
Our work is the result of three years' worth of hard-work and determination.
