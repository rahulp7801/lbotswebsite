The world is powered by open knowledge. It is the shared heritage of all of humanity, and we wish to not break from this amazing tradition.

We believe that keeping knowledge to a few people is not the way to go. So, we love to share information with our community to help the world become a better place.
As today's information becomes yesterday's history, it might be forgotten, perhaps even lost. Therefore, sharing information across multiple people helps preserve the valuable knowledge that the human race has learned over the years.
