Project

In a challenging and different year, the Lightning Bots, a team of 9, came together to create a project that made exercise fun.
Project Statement
How can we motivate people to exercise?
Project Constraints & Criteria
We wanted our project to be very tech-savvy, affordable for the average person, and improve overall health while encouraging people to exercise.
Project Summary
We are currently developing a software that converts videos into video games. You can "ReLive" your past experiences as if they were actually happening.
Efficient and practical.
The Lightning Bots model their strategies on being both practical and efficient. This is clearly evident in their robot game, in which they were able to achieve nearly 500 points.
