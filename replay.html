Replay Project
Before we get into explaining our Replay Project, let's get on to knowing what exactly this season is about. As you probably know this years season is called RePlay, if you didn't know that well you know now! This years season is abut helping people to become more active, and motivating them to be more active!

We were split on multiple ideas for this years season. Somone of them were motivating people to exercise, making sure excersie is acessible to all, and making exercise available to the disabled. When we were finalizing what our goal and what problem we looked aorund to see what people were doing. We noticed lots of people were spending time indoors and spending hours upon hours using electroncis and playing video games. Inspired by that we wanted to tackle the problem of motivating more people to exercise as it was a widespread problem during these times, and it has an overall bigger impact on the majority of the population.

To make people more motivated we decided to make an app. Our will have many features, to help people exercise. You can choose from 3 sections, Sports, Workouts and Adventure.
